About

Who We Are

ENERSUN SDN. BHD.

Enersun Sdn Bhd (ENERSUN Co.Ltd) is a proudly Malaysian-owned company specializing in the manufacturing and wholesale distribution of high-quality LED lighting products since 2009. In 2012, we established state-of-the-art LED production facilities in Sungai Petani, Kedah, Malaysia. Demonstrating our dedication to excellence and compliance with industry standards, we became one of the first lighting companies in Malaysia to receive the prestigious SIRIM certification in 2013.

Our flagship products include LED downlights, LED floodlights, LED high bay lights, LED street lights, LED tubes, LED bulbs, and a wide range of other lighting solutions, meticulously crafted for both residential and industrial use. In addition to our lighting products, we also offer a diverse selection of high-performance lighting apparatus, drivers, and essential components.

We take immense pride in our commitment to quality. Most of our products meet stringent IEC standards and are rigorously tested by top-tier laboratories, including TUV and SIRIM QAS, ensuring superior durability, efficiency, and reliability.
