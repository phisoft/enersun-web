Who We Are

ENERSUN SDN. BHD.

Enersun is one of Malaysias’s leading LED Lighting manufacturers specialising in high quality LED lighting solutions. Enersun is based in Malaysia and has been operating since 2009, providing high quality lighting products. In 2012, Enersun started its own LED product lines both in ZhongSan City, GuangDong China, and in Sungai Petani, Kedah Malaysia. As a trusted home brand, “ENERSUN” has been registered in Malaysia since 2009 and trademarked in China during 2013.

Our main products are LED Down Lights, LED Flood Lights, LED High Bay Lights, LED Street Lights, LED Tubes, and various energy saving bulbs and lighting apparatus, for both residential and industrial use. We also manufacture various types of LED drivers and related parts. The majority of our products have relevant IEC standards, and all products sold in Malaysia must go through consignment testing by laboratory SIRIM QAS International Berhad.

Policies

Quality Policies

IEC Standardized

All of our LED products have met IEC standards which are standardized by the IECEE scheme. For energy saving bulbs, we have met IEC 60968 and IEC 60969 standards which are standardized by the IECEE scheme as well. SIRIM QAS Laboratory is our partner in doing product tests in order to meet the relevant standards.

Our products also undergo our own in-house quality control processes and product checks to ensure that manufacturing meets the company’s “high quality brand” objectives.
